About me

Hello, my name is Leo and I am a creative soul and an hobby artist. I am into various creative disciplines, be it knitting, sewing, pottery, crocheting or macrame.

With my projects I want to capture the beauty and diversity of the world and its different cultures and facets.

As a web design and development student, I have the opportunity to combine my creative passion with my technical expertise. The digital world offers endless opportunities to bring my ideas to life. Every project is an exciting challenge for me, through which I can expand my skills and perspective.

I invite you to join me on my journey as I continue to evolve and experience new creative adventures. Maybe we can even discover the magic of art and design together. Let's be inspired and create together!

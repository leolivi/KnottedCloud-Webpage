Community

In our community, creativity knows no boundaries. We'd love to welcome you to our vibrant and inspiring crafting community. It doesn't matter what craft you do, and whether you're an experienced artist or a beginner looking to explore your creative side, our community provides a space for everyone to interact, share ideas, and bring new projects to life. If you're in Basel and want to join, contact me anytime!

Join the community!

Community values

Supportive environment

We know the ups and downs that come with crafting. We support and motivate each other, celebrate each other's successes, offer help, and share our ideas with each other. Your work deserves to be seen and celebrated!

Collaborations

Even more enjoyable than creating something of your own is creating it together! We bring our skills and ideas together to create something new - and most importantly, to have fun.

Diverse Crafting

We all have different crafting interests and strengths. From sewing and knitting to macrame and drawing, you'll find people who are passionate about different crafts. This diversity brings new perspectives and ideas and inspires new creations and collaborations.

Learning Opportunities

We believe in continuous growth and learning. Our community regularly meets in person, exchanges knowledge and offers workshops. Whether you want to improve your skills or learn something completely new, we have a lot of knowledge waiting to be shared with you.
